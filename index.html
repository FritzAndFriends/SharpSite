About SharpSite

SharpSite is an open-source content management system built with .NET and Blazor technologies. Developed live on Twitch, SharpSite aims to provide a flexible and powerful platform for creating and managing web content.

Purpose

The purpose of the SharpSite content management system is to empower developers and content creators with a robust, scalable, and easy-to-use platform. SharpSite is designed to streamline the process of building and maintaining websites, offering a wide range of features and customization options to meet the needs of various projects. Whether you're creating a personal blog, a corporate website, or an e-commerce platform, SharpSite provides the tools and flexibility to bring your vision to life.
